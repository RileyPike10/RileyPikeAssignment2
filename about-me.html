My Interests

I love working on cars, whether it’s repairing, modifying, or just learning about their mechanics. Along with that comes the thrill of riding motorcycles, it’s an incredible way to experience the open road and makes life better.

I'm also interested in video games, both playing and creating them. Game development allows me to combine creativity with problem-solving, and I enjoy bringing my ideas to life and allow other to experience what I create.

When I want to relax, I enjoy reading. Whether it's technical books, sci-fi, or classic literature, reading helps me expand my knowledge and imagination and is the best form of meditation in my opinion.
